Vehicle Management Systems (VMS) - Fuel Management Subsystem

Below is a collection of Ptolemy II models illustrating various modeling issues with cyber-physical systems (CPSs), using a portion of a vehicle-management systems (VMS) that manages multiple fuel tanks in an aircraft as an example. The details of how these models illustrate modeling issues are covered in the paper:

Patricia Derler, Edward A. Lee, Alberto Sangiovanni-Vincentelli. Modeling Cyber-Physical Systems, Proceedings of the IEEE (special issue on CPS), 100(1):13-28, January 2012.

That paper includes hyperlinks to some of these models.
